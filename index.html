Tap, explore, unwind

Interactive Scenes

Explore immersive interactive calming scenes on iPhone and iPad — fluid motion, gentle visuals, and playful touch interaction built for sensory-friendly focus, ADHD calm, anxiety relief, and everyday stress relief. Each scene responds to tap, double tap, and drag so you can match the pace your nervous system needs. Tap any preview below for fullscreen and sound.

  • Single tap — one gentle response at a time. Best when you want slow, steady calm and predictable sensory feedback.
  • Double tap — the same interaction, faster. Best when you want more motion and energy without leaving the scene.
  • Drag — explore with your finger and let the scene follow you. Best for tracing, wandering, and settled hands-on focus.
